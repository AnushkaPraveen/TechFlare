What is CODE:RUSH

INTECS initiated the coderush competition to find your hidden skills in problem solving and coding. In this overnight hackathon you need to solve challenges as a team which are published in hackerrank platform by our experienced problem setters. These challenges are from ad-hoc, arrays, stack/queue, grahp, string manipulation, bit manipulation, recursion, dynamic programming, and mathematical algorithms categories. This grand opportunity bestowed only to the members of FIT family. So get the most out of this event. Happy Coding.
