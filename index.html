What is CODE:RUSH

INTECS initiated the coderush competition to find your hidden skills in problem solving and coding. In this overnight hackathon you need to solve challenges as a team which are published in hackerrank platform by our experienced problem setters. These challenges are from ad-hoc, arrays, stack/queue, grahp, string manipulation, bit manipulation, recursion, dynamic programming, and mathematical algorithms categories. This grand opportunity bestowed only to the members of FIT family. So get the most out of this event. Happy Coding.

The Way you must follow

Development Path

1st Week

  • Make a 1 min presentation to present the problem you address, your approach and technology which will be adopted
  • Design high level architecture and UI wireframes for the system.

2nd Week

  • Separate modules and allocate tasks to the members via any project management platform (Clickup, Jira, trello).
  • Initiate github repository and the version controlling environment.
  • Initiate developments and define the first releasing module.

3rd Week

  • Complete first module including relevant unit testing and publish duly completed module through GitHub.
  • Take a screenshot of your scrum meeting with your members and mentors and post in LinkedIn.

4th Week

  • Complete 40% of the system and publish them through GitHub.

5th Week

  • Complete 70% of the system and publish them through GitHub.
  • Tea with Brotherhood Stack.
    • Have discussion with another team who follows the same tech stack.
    • Take a screenshot of party and post in LinkedIn.

6th Week

  • Complete 100% of the system and publish them through GitHub

7th Week

  • Integrate modules and test the system.
  • Create a system testing report via github.

8th Week

  • Publish duly completed version through GitHub.
  • Make 1 min video to introduce your program.
  • Celebrate your success with team members and mentors. Do not forget to share your story via LinkedIn.
The following are the ground rules for CodeRush 2020

Rules and Regulations

Team Details

  • Single team should include a minimum of 3 members and maximum 4 members.
  • A team leader will be appointed for each team.
  • The team leader will maintain constant communication with the mentor.
  • The team leader will monitor the progress of the project.
  • Frequent communication and progress updates with the mentor must be ensured.
  • Tasks must be delegated among the team members to ensure each member shares equal responsibility.

Benifits

“Tea with CTO” sessions for selected top 5 groups.

Arrange an interactive session with industry partners and share experience for further development of the project.
